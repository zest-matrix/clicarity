Questions

Simple Answers to What You're
Probably Wondering Right Now.

Why only two screens — is that really enough?
Yes — and that's exactly the point. Most tracking systems fail on the shop floor because they're too complex. Too many fields, too many logins, too much to remember. Clicarity's two-screen design means any floor team member can start using it in under a minute, with zero training. The complexity lives in the dashboard — where it belongs. Not on the floor.
We're already using WhatsApp for everything. Will this disrupt that?
No — it improves it. Clicarity integrates with WhatsApp so your team still gets updates there, but now they're automatic and structured. Instead of a flood of messages asking for status, the right person gets a clean notification the moment a job reaches their stage. Same channel. Far less noise. Zero manual coordination.
We already have an ERP. Do we have to replace it?
Not at all. Clicarity is not an ERP — it does one thing ERPs don't do well: live job tracking on the floor in real time. It plugs alongside your existing ERP via a live two-way API sync. No double entry. No disruption. You keep what works and add what's missing.
Will my floor staff actually use this? They're not tech-savvy.
This is the most common concern — and the one that gets answered first in every demo. The update screen has exactly two fields: a Job ID and a Status. Nothing else. A team member on the factory floor with zero tech experience can use it in under 60 seconds. Clicarity adapts to your language and terminology — your team never changes how they work.
Who sets this up? Do I need an IT team?
The Clicarity team handles everything. You tell us your process — your stages, your terminology, how work moves through your operation. We configure the entire system for you. No IT involvement. No disruption to your operations. No configuration from your end. You go live in days, not months.
How does pricing work? Is there a per-user cost?
No per-user charges. Ever. Clicarity is billed as an annual licence for your business. Add your entire team, your floor staff, your vendors — the cost stays the same. Single licence for one process or location, multi-licence for businesses with multiple processes or locations.
Can vendors and outside partners use it too?
Yes. Vendors get their own login and can only see and update their assigned jobs. Their updates flow directly into your live dashboard — without any calls, follow-ups, or coordination effort on your side. They see what's relevant to them. You see everything.
Is our business data safe?
Completely. Clicarity runs on AWS Cloud with a PostgreSQL database — enterprise-grade infrastructure. We do not store your data beyond what your process requires. We don't sell it. We don't access it. Your data belongs entirely to your business — always.
