Where Education Businesses Lose Learners & Revenue

Real bottlenecks. Real costs. Real fixes.

These are the patterns Clicarity helps education training businesses identify and fix within the first few weeks.

Assignment Non-Submission
12 learners not submitted for 3 weeks — discovered when they emailed to drop out
A professional training institute tracked assignment submission status per learner. 12 out of 48 learners in a cohort had not submitted for 3 consecutive weeks — but the programme team only discovered this when several emailed to say they were dropping out. With Clicarity, non-submission flags after 7 days. Proactive outreach made. Drop-out rate in the following cohort: reduced by 60%.
Drop-out rate reduced 60% — at-risk learners identified early
Grading Backlog
Faculty grading backlog causing 2-week delay in feedback — learners demotivated
A training programme tracked time between assignment submission and feedback delivery. Average time was 14 days — faculty were grading multiple cohorts simultaneously without a clear system. Learners were completing the next module without feedback on the previous one. With Clicarity, grading is a tracked stage with a 5-day target. Average feedback time: 4 days.
Feedback time reduced from 14 days to 4 days
Certificate Delay
Certificates not issued 6 weeks after programme end — learners asking repeatedly
A training institute found that certificate issuance was consistently 5-7 weeks after programme completion — the process required multiple approvals that nobody was tracking. With Clicarity, certificate issuance is a stage that begins immediately after results are declared. Each approval step is visible. Average certificate issuance time: 8 days.
Certificate issuance reduced from 5-7 weeks to 8 days

Questions from education training businesses

How does Clicarity work for a printing business specifically?
Clicarity is configured around your specific print workflow — pre-press, printing, finishing, dispatch — in your terminology. Every job gets a unique ID and moves through your defined stages. You see live status, timestamps, and bottleneck alerts at every stage. The Clicarity team builds everything for you. You go live in 5 days.
Does it work for both offset and digital printing?
Yes. Clicarity works for any print process — offset, digital, wide format, screen printing, or a combination. The stages are configured around your actual workflow. If offset jobs go through plate making and digital jobs skip that stage, both can be tracked correctly under the same system.
Can it track material wastage at each stage?
Yes — with the Multi Licence. At every stage, your team logs quantity in and quantity out. Clicarity calculates the loss automatically. You see wastage by stage, by job, live — not at month-end when nothing can be done about it.
Will my press operators actually use it?
Yes. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and status. Under a minute. No training needed. GP Offset's entire team was using it from day one, including floor workers who had never used a software system before.
