Your Production Flow

Every job. Every approval. Every roll tracked.

This is how a print job moves through your business. Clicarity gives you live visibility at every phase — and flags delays before they become client calls.

01
Order & Artwork
4 stages
Order Received
Customer order confirmed — label spec, substrate, quantity, finish, deadline.
✓ Clicarity: Job ID created, artwork brief logged, approval process initiated
Artwork Received / Created
Artwork received from customer or created by studio.
⚠ ⚠ Common delay: artwork errors found late — back-and-forth adds days
Colour Proof / Digital Proof
Proof sent to customer for colour and content approval.
⚠ ⚠ Common delay: customer takes 3-7 days to approve — job sits idle
Plate / Cylinder Making
Printing plates or cylinders made after artwork approval.
✓ Clicarity: Approval locked before plate-making — no production without sign-off
02
Substrate & Setup
3 stages
Substrate Procurement
Label stock, film, or foil procured or pulled from inventory.
⚠ ⚠ Common delay: substrate out of stock — job waits while material is sourced
Press Setup
Flexo, digital, or offset press set up for the job.
⚠ ⚠ Common delay: colour matching issues on setup — waste before production
Make-Ready / Trial
Trial run conducted, colour and register checked.
✓ Clicarity: Make-ready waste logged — excess flagged against standard
03
Production
5 stages
Printing
Labels printed on roll or sheet.
✓ Clicarity: Linear metres or quantity tracked — wastage visible live
UV Varnish / Lamination
Protective or decorative finish applied.
⚠ ⚠ Common delay: lamination machine queue — jobs sitting between stages
Hot / Cold Foiling
Foil stamping applied where required.
Die Cutting / Punching
Labels cut to shape using die or punch.
✓ Clicarity: Rejection rate at die cutting tracked per run
Slitting / Rewinding
Labels slit to customer-specified width and wound on cores.
✓ Clicarity: Final metres vs ordered metres confirmed before dispatch
04
Quality & Dispatch
3 stages
Quality Inspection
Labels inspected for colour, print quality, die cut accuracy.
✓ Clicarity: QC result logged — defect pattern visible across jobs
Packing & Labelling
Rolls or sheets packed with job details, quantity, and customer labels.
Dispatch
Job dispatched with delivery challan.
✓ Clicarity: Full job history from order to delivery preserved
Where Label Printers Lose Jobs & Margin

Real bottlenecks. Real costs. Real fixes.

These are the patterns Clicarity helps businesses identify and fix within the first few weeks.

Colour Approval
Jobs waiting 6 days for approval — deadline missed before production even started
A label printer tracked time from proof sent to approval received. Average approval time: 6.2 days. Clicarity flagged jobs after 2 days. Proactive follow-up reduced average approval time to 1.8 days. On-time delivery improved 40%.
Approval cycle reduced from 6.2 to 1.8 days
Substrate Waste
Make-ready waste running 22% above standard — nobody measuring it
Tracking waste at press setup revealed make-ready waste was 22% of total substrate consumption on short runs. Root cause: inconsistent colour matching procedures. Standard process documented. Make-ready waste reduced to 8%.
Make-ready waste reduced from 22% to 8%
Die Cut Rejection
8% rejection at die cutting — same die, same problem every month
Stage-wise rejection tracking showed die cutting rejection was 8% consistently — on the same die every month. The die was worn but nobody had raised it because the overall numbers looked manageable. Die replaced. Rejection dropped to 0.8%.
Die cutting rejection reduced from 8% to 0.8%

Questions from label printing businesses

How does Clicarity work for a printing business specifically?
Clicarity is configured around your specific print workflow — pre-press, printing, finishing, dispatch — in your terminology. Every job gets a unique ID and moves through your defined stages. You see live status, timestamps, and bottleneck alerts at every stage. The Clicarity team builds everything for you. You go live in 5 days.
Does it work for both offset and digital printing?
Yes. Clicarity works for any print process — offset, digital, wide format, screen printing, or a combination. The stages are configured around your actual workflow. If offset jobs go through plate making and digital jobs skip that stage, both can be tracked correctly under the same system.
Can it track material wastage at each stage?
Yes — with the Multi Licence. At every stage, your team logs quantity in and quantity out. Clicarity calculates the loss automatically. You see wastage by stage, by job, live — not at month-end when nothing can be done about it.
Will my press operators actually use it?
Yes. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and status. Under a minute. No training needed. GP Offset's entire team was using it from day one, including floor workers who had never used a software system before.
