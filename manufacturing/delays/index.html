Questions

How does Clicarity help with production delays?
Clicarity tracks every job through every stage with timestamps. When a job sits too long at a stage, it's visible on your dashboard. You see the delay forming — not after the deadline is missed.
Will my floor workers actually use it?
Yes. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and Status. Under a minute. No training needed. A floor worker who has never used a smartphone can do it.
Do we need to change our process?
No. Clicarity is built around your existing stages. Your team does what they already do — they just update status at each step. The Clicarity team configures everything. You go live in 5 days.
Is our production data safe?
Yes. AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit. Hosted on AWS EC2 and S3, stored in PostgreSQL on Oracle servers. ISO 27001 certification in progress. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
