Questions

Everything You Want to Know
Before Booking.

What exactly is Clicarity?
Clicarity is a cloud-based job process tracking system. It tracks how every job moves through your business — from creation to completion — giving your entire team live visibility without any calls or follow-ups. It runs on AWS with a PostgreSQL database, making it enterprise-grade in reliability while being simple enough for the factory floor.
How is this different from an ERP?
ERPs manage everything — accounting, inventory, HR, payroll. Clicarity does one thing exceptionally well: tracking how jobs move through your process stages in real time. That's why it's faster to adopt, simpler to use, and actually gets used on the shop floor. Most businesses already have an ERP — Clicarity plugs in alongside it through a live API sync, so there's no double entry and no disruption.
Does my team need to learn new software?
No. The update screen has just two fields — a Job ID and a Status. A team member on the factory floor with zero technology experience can use it in under a minute. Clicarity adapts to your language and your terminology. Your team never changes how they work — they just have a better system underneath them.
We coordinate heavily on WhatsApp. Will this disrupt that?
Not at all — it improves it. Clicarity integrates with WhatsApp so your team still gets updates there, but now they're automatic and structured. Instead of a flood of messages asking for status, the right person gets a clean notification the moment a job reaches their stage. Same channel. Far less noise.
Can vendors and external partners use it?
Yes. Vendors get their own login and can only see and update their assigned jobs. Their updates flow directly into your live dashboard — without any calls, follow-ups, or coordination effort from your side.
How does billing work? Is there a per-user cost?
No per-user charges. Clicarity is billed as an annual licence for your business. Add as many team members as your operation needs — your cost stays the same. Single licence for one process or location, multi-licence for businesses with multiple processes or teams.
Who handles the setup? Do we need an IT team?
The Clicarity team handles everything. You share your process stages, your terminology, and your current job format. We configure the entire system around your workflow. No IT involvement. No disruption to your operations. No configuration on your end.
Is our business data secure?
Completely. Clicarity runs on AWS Cloud with a PostgreSQL database — enterprise-grade infrastructure. We do not store your data beyond what your process requires, we don't sell it, and we don't access it. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
