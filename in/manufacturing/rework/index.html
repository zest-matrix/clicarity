Questions

How does Clicarity help reduce rework?
At every stage, your team logs what was processed and what was rejected. Clicarity tracks this across all jobs and shows you which stage has the highest rejection rate — so you can fix the root cause rather than just accepting the cost.
How quickly will rework reduce?
The data starts building from day one. Within 2-4 weeks you have enough job history to see clear patterns. Once you can see which stage is causing the most rework, you can fix the process — and the same error stops repeating.
We use Tally. Does Clicarity work alongside it?
Yes. Tally manages your accounts. Clicarity tracks your production. They do completely different things and work side by side. No double entry. No disruption. A floor worker who has never used a smartphone can do it.

Yes. AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit. AWS EC2 and S3, PostgreSQL on Oracle servers. ISO 27001 in progress. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
