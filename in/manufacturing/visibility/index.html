Questions

How does Clicarity give me live floor visibility?
Every time a worker updates a job status, your dashboard updates instantly. You can open it from any device and see every job, its current stage, and how long it's been there — without being on the floor.
We use Tally. Does Clicarity work with it?
Yes. Tally handles your accounts. Clicarity tracks your production. They work side by side — no double entry, no disruption.
Do workers need training?
No. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and Status. Under a minute. No training needed. A floor worker who has never used a smartphone can do it.
Is our data safe?
Yes. AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit. AWS EC2 and S3, PostgreSQL on Oracle servers. ISO 27001 in progress. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
