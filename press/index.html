Founder

The person behind Clicarity

PR
Puneet Rawat
Founder & CEO — Clicarity
Mumbai-based printpreneur with nearly two decades in the printing and packaging industry. As a student in the United States, Puneet observed systems and dignity of labour that India's factory floors lacked. Back in India, he saw the same communication gap everywhere: owners making 12 calls before 10am, supervisors with no real answers, workers doing honest work without visibility to show for it.

In June 2025, he founded Clicarity — a lightweight production tracking system designed to give every factory owner real-time visibility of their shop floor, without the complexity of traditional ERP. The system is built around one belief: "The team is not weak. They work hard. It is the system that is missing."

The road ahead

Clicarity's long-term goal is to reach 700–800 clients across India's printing, packaging, and manufacturing sectors — and to achieve an SME IPO (small and medium enterprises public offering) by 2030.

The vision is one of inclusivity and professional growth. By digitising traditionally noisy environments with streamlined interfaces, the industry becomes more attractive to a generation that expects digital upgrades. This transition also provides a path to empower workers by creating clear, system-based communication that bypasses traditional ego-driven hurdles.

Clicarity has already secured several clients across printing and manufacturing sectors. The company offers three to six-month trials to prove that visibility leads to tangible results.
