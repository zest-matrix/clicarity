Where Industrial Projects Lose Time & Cost Control

Real bottlenecks. Real costs. Real fixes.

These are the patterns Clicarity helps businesses identify and fix within the first few weeks.

Equipment Delivery
Critical pump delivered 6 weeks late — civil complete and idle for 6 weeks
An expediting log was maintained in spreadsheets with no visibility to the project team. A critical pump delivery slipped 6 weeks — nobody escalated because the delay was not visible at the project level. Civil was complete and the erection crew was idle. With Clicarity, each equipment delivery has a status and a flag date. Escalation triggered at week 2.
Equipment delivery escalated at week 2 — not week 6
Drawing Revision
Fabrication done to superseded drawing — ₹4.2L rework
A structural fabricator used a drawing that had been superseded by a revision issued the previous day. Fabrication was 60% complete when the error was found. Rework cost: ₹4.2L. With Clicarity, drawing revision is a tracked stage — fabricators see only the current approved drawing. Revision-related rework: eliminated.
₹4.2L revision rework prevented — drawing control enforced
Status Meetings
3-hour weekly project review replaced by 20-minute exception review
A project team was spending 3 hours every week in status meetings — each package manager reporting individually. Most of the meeting covered items that were on track. With Clicarity, the project director sees all package statuses before the meeting. Meetings now focus only on flagged items. Meeting time reduced from 3 hours to 20 minutes.

Questions from industrial projects businesses

How does Clicarity work for a printing business specifically?
Clicarity is configured around your specific print workflow — pre-press, printing, finishing, dispatch — in your terminology. Every job gets a unique ID and moves through your defined stages. You see live status, timestamps, and bottleneck alerts at every stage. The Clicarity team builds everything for you. You go live in 5 days.
Does it work for both offset and digital printing?
Yes. Clicarity works for any print process — offset, digital, wide format, screen printing, or a combination. The stages are configured around your actual workflow. If offset jobs go through plate making and digital jobs skip that stage, both can be tracked correctly under the same system.
Can it track material wastage at each stage?
Yes — with the Multi Licence. At every stage, your team logs quantity in and quantity out. Clicarity calculates the loss automatically. You see wastage by stage, by job, live — not at month-end when nothing can be done about it.
Will my press operators actually use it?
Yes. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and status. Under a minute. No training needed. GP Offset's entire team was using it from day one, including floor workers who had never used a software system before.
