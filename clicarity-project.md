## Product Positioning (LOCKED)

Clicarity is NOT described as:
- Job tracking software
- ERP
- CRM
- Project management tool

Clicarity IS described as:
- Bottleneck Identifier
- Delay Identifier
- Profit Protector (between processes)
- Live process and workflow visibility system
- Operational clarity tool
- Stage-by-stage accountability system

**Core product truth:** Tracks any job, order, task, or process through any defined sequence of stages. Shows what is moving, what is stuck, what is delayed — live, on one screen, for the entire team. Does not do accounts, inventory, HR, invoicing, or calculations.

**Hero tagline (LOCKED):** "Solve JSR & TAT — The Two Biggest Operational Problems in Any Growing Business"

**Pricing (LOCKED):**
- Single Licence: ₹30,000/year · One process · Unlimited users
- Multi Licence: ₹55,000/year · Multiple processes · Unlimited users · Component tracking · Webhooks
- Add-ons: WhatsApp Alerts · Vendor Portal
- Go live: 5 days
- No per-user fees. No hidden charges.

**No ERP or CRM language anywhere on the website.**
