Questions

How does Clicarity track material wastage?
At every stage, your team enters quantity in and quantity out. Clicarity calculates the loss automatically and shows it live on your dashboard. You see which stage is losing material while the job is still running — not at month-end.
Will my floor workers actually use it?
Yes. The update screen has three fields — Job ID, quantity in, quantity out. Under a minute. No training needed. A floor worker who has never used a smartphone can do it.
We use Tally for accounts. Does Clicarity replace it?
Nahi. Clicarity aapke existing stages ke around configure hoti hai. Tally ke saath bhi kaam karti hai. Clicarity team sab setup karti hai. 24 ghante mein live.
Is our production data safe?
Yes. AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS in transit. Hosted on AWS EC2 and S3, stored in PostgreSQL on Oracle servers. ISO 27001 certification in progress. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
