Top Ten Takeaways About Clicarity

PrintWeek India's editorial team distilled the Clicarity story into ten points — published in the April 2026 edition.

01
Launch & Reception
Launched June 2025. Printers appreciated simplicity — track components and wastage without a complex ERP.
02
Core Problem Solved
Resolves heavy reliance on phone calls, verbal updates, and manual follow-ups with a simple digital layer for real-time job visibility.
03
Development Foundation
Built over three years, rooted in nearly two decades of experience observing communication gaps in printing and packaging.
04
Difference from ERP
Lightweight, faster to adopt, focused purely on production workflow visibility — not finance or inventory.
05
Ease of Use
Requires the least tech-savvy operator to select a Job ID and update status via a minimal, relevant-field-only interface.
06
Primary Tracking
The Job ID is the central mechanism — automatically recording user, process, date, and time to establish a clear production trail.
07
Component Tracking
Tracks jobs with multiple components — each tracked separately through its own production sequence.
08
Wastage Visibility
Lot-wise recording of actual quantity and wastage at each stage — clear visibility into where losses occur in the chain.
09
Vendor Integration
Outsourced jobs can be assigned to vendors during an update. Vendors get WhatsApp notifications and optional dashboard access.
10
Adaptability & Support
Flexible fields adapt to any company's workflow. Dedicated WhatsApp group for coordination and remote assistance.

  • Bottleneck alerts flag any job stagnating at a specific process
  • Management has full pipeline visibility on one screen
