Where Ceramic Manufacturers Lose Batches & Margin

Real bottlenecks. Real costs. Real fixes.

These are the patterns Clicarity helps businesses identify and fix within the first few weeks.

Kiln Deviation
Kiln temperature deviation creating 40% second grade — found at grading
A tile manufacturer tracked kiln temperature logs against grade outcomes. Batches where kiln temperature deviated by more than 8°C had 38-42% second grade output. With Clicarity, kiln temperature is a logged stage. Deviations flag in real time. Second grade from kiln deviation reduced 45%.
Second grade from kiln deviation reduced 45%
Moisture Issue
Spray dryer moisture deviation causing warpage — discovered after firing
Spray dryer outlet moisture was not being logged consistently. High moisture batches were being pressed and fired — warpage discovered only at grading. With Clicarity, moisture logging is a mandatory stage before pressing. Warpage rejections from moisture deviation: eliminated.
Warpage from moisture deviation eliminated — pre-press check enforced
Dispatch Short
Order dispatched with wrong grade mix — retailer complained
A dispatch team packed B grade tiles in an A grade order box. Retailer discovered the mix and returned the full consignment — ₹2.1L in logistics and restocking cost. With Clicarity, packing requires grade confirmation against the order. Grade mix incidents: zero since implementation.
₹2.1L grade mix return prevented — grade confirmation enforced at packing

Questions from ceramic tiles businesses

How does Clicarity work for a printing business specifically?
Clicarity is configured around your specific print workflow — pre-press, printing, finishing, dispatch — in your terminology. Every job gets a unique ID and moves through your defined stages. You see live status, timestamps, and bottleneck alerts at every stage. The Clicarity team builds everything for you. You go live in 5 days.
Does it work for both offset and digital printing?
Yes. Clicarity works for any print process — offset, digital, wide format, screen printing, or a combination. The stages are configured around your actual workflow. If offset jobs go through plate making and digital jobs skip that stage, both can be tracked correctly under the same system.
Can it track material wastage at each stage?
Yes — with the Multi Licence. At every stage, your team logs quantity in and quantity out. Clicarity calculates the loss automatically. You see wastage by stage, by job, live — not at month-end when nothing can be done about it.
Will my press operators actually use it?
