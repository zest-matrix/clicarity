Questions

How does Clicarity fix shift handover gaps?
Every job shows its current stage and how long it has been there — live. When the incoming shift starts, they open one screen and immediately see what's pending, what's urgent, and what was completed. No briefing call needed.
Does it work for night shifts too?
Yes. Clicarity runs 24/7. Every update is timestamped regardless of when it happens. The night shift's work is fully visible to the morning shift the moment they start — and vice versa.
Do workers need training?
No. The update screen has two fields — Job ID and Status. Under a minute. No training needed. A floor worker who has never used a smartphone can do it.
Is our data safe?
Yes. AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit. AWS EC2 and S3, PostgreSQL on Oracle servers. ISO 27001 in progress. Your data belongs entirely to your business.
